Local Delicacies

Buko Pie

The filling of this pie is made with young coconuts, sometimes along with sweetened condensed milk which makes it creamy and sweet. It is popular as a food for travelers to take back home.

Halo-halo

A cold dessert featuring a mixture of sweet ingredients, hence the name "Halo-halo". It is a perfect delicacy to beat the heat from the tropical climate.

Pichi-pichi

A form of a cassava cake usually covered in grated coconut and cheese, and can come in many different flavors.
