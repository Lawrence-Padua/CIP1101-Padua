My Bucket List

1. Vigan City, Ilocos Sur

As a UNESCO world heritage site, this tourist destination features a blast from the past with the well-preserved architecture from the Spanish period, combined with the local culture and cuisine of the Ilocanos.

2. Sagada, Mountain Province

This tourist destination located in the mountains provides a glimpse into the culture of the locals with the hanging coffins, in addition to the cool climate and the caves and waterfalls in the area.

3. Coron, Palawan

Coron features white sand beaches, crystal clear waters where you can see coral reefs. More importantly, it features Kayangan Lake, known for being one of the cleanest lakes in the country as well as having a breathtaking scenery around it.

4. Puerto Princesa, Palawan

Puerto Princesa has a lot of famous tourist attractions, such as the underground river, where stalactites, stalagmites, and bats can be found. It also features eco-tours with mangrove forests featuring diverse wildlife.

What About Outside the Country?

1. Eiffel Tower, France

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol found in Paris, France. It is 324 meters tall and provides an observation deck at its top. More importantly, it is quite the spectacle at night with its dazzling lights.

2. Niagara Falls, Canada

The Niagara Falls are not one big waterfall, but are composed of 3 smaller waterfalls next to each other. Its main spectacle is at night, when the falls would get illuminated by colorful lights which add to the already grand waterfalls.
